Social Sports Dublin
A friendly, diverse and welcoming organisation promoting community through sport and exercise

A friendly welcoming group who play mixed social and competitive Ultimate Frisbee and regularly host additional social events alongside our sports groups.

Want to socialise? Want exercise? Play with us! All welcome - Men, women, beginners and advanced!

As a non-contact sport, it is perfect for beginners and advanced, men and women, to all play together. Don’t worry if you have never played before! It only takes a few minutes to learn the basics of the sport and we have a great mix of experience levels, from first timers to those who have been playing for years, to help you along the way.

Play occasionally or play often. We have a very international group from all over the world and always love to see new faces! It’s a fun way to meet new people. Most of our group didn’t know each other before joining but are now great friends.

We play in ALL WEATHERS and some of us even love playing in the rain! Don’t worry if only a few people have signed up through Meetup. We have a very large group of players in our WhatsApp group that come regularly and we ALWAYS have enough for a game!

About Us

Social Sports Dublin is a Dublin-based social group that meets several times weekly to play a game called ultimate Frisbee. We’re a beginner friendly group that has introduced over 200 people to the game since we started in 2020. In addition to ultimate Frisbee we also do various social activities like paintball, axe throwing, horse racing, pizza and beer nights at local pubs, volunteer, and plenty of other fun things. Our membership consists of both men and women ranging from ages 20-40, representing over a dozen countries from around the world. You might also know us from our competitive ultimate Frisbee team, Sundrive Social Ultimate. This team plays in tournaments hosted across Ireland, which is a fun way to stay connected to other Frisbee clubs across the country. If you’re looking for a great group of people that are active and enjoy trying new things, we’d love to meet you!

Social Aspects

In addition to both the casual and competitive side of Ultimate Frisbee in the club, the easing of Covid restrictions throughout 2021 also saw a rise in the number of social events we were able to attend. About Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate is a non-contact, self-refereed team sport played with a flying disc frisbee. The aim of the game: Two teams of seven players compete on a playing field about the same length as a football field, but narrower. At each end of the playing field there is an end zone. Each team defends one end zone. They score a goal if one of their players catches the disc in the opposite end zone. The player with the disc is called the thrower. The thrower may not run with the disc. Instead they move the disc by passing to team-mates in any direction. The defensive team gets possession of the disc if an offensive team’s throw is not caught by a player of the same team. Then the defensive team becomes the offensive team and can try to score in the opposite end zone.

Spirit of the Game: Ultimate relies upon a Spirit of the Game that places the responsibility for fair play on every player. There are no referees; the players are solely responsible for following and enforcing the rules, even at World Championship. Competitive play is encouraged, but never at the expense of respect between players, adherence to the rules, and the basic joy of play.

More info on ultimate frisbee check out: https://www.rulesofsport.com/sports/ultimate-frisbee.html

Practice Info

Sessions run each Saturday 2-4pm in Eamonn Ceannt Park, Dublin. We play in all weathers!

You can also check out our Meetup for times and locations.

What to expect

Don't worry if you've never played ultimate before, teaching people the game is our speciality. Experienced players are more than welcome too!

We invite new people to join us on Saturdays, which is our beginner orientated session and a great opportunity to meet the group and give the game a try. We'll also invite you to join our WhatsApp group where we share updates, coordinate socials and joke around.

We also train on Mondays and Wednesdays, those sessions are a little more advanced. Ask us for training details in person.

Cost

€5 per session or €50 for the season. First session is free!

What to bring

  • Water
  • Wear clothes you're comfortable running in
  • Footwear for running on grass. Ideally football boots, but runners will do
  • A smile!
